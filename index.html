About our company

Project tech is a new age Interest-based Social Networking platform from India, where people share their beautiful life stories & skills in their interests. With our "Privacy-first" social platform and hyper local features, people can safely connect with nearby, like-minded, authentic profiles. These authentic user networks & nearby recommendations help people to create offline communities based on their interests.

Project tech is simply the safest social network app to share your stories with your loved ones and with the world. It is built from India for the world with its unique and new user experience, culture, values, and all other features.
